Chinmaya Mission Motto

Our Leaders

Chinmaya Mission® was established in India in 1953 by devotees of the world-renowned Vedanta teacher, His Holiness Swami Chinmayananda, a true visionary, whose life was dedicated to selfless service to others. Since then the Mission has encompassed a wide range of spiritual, educational, and charitable activities, ennobling the lives of millions in India and all over the world. His Holiness Swami Tejomayananda, took over as the spiritual head of Chinmaya Mission® worldwide in 1993. Under his leadership and guidance many new centers have been established and the Mission continues to grow in all spheres of its activities, providing enrichment to the society at large.

Chinmaya Mission Centers : Many services , One Goal

There are more than 300 Chinmaya Mission centers worldwide, each a “spiritual home” providing service and enrichment to society by having the following programs:

  • Bala Vihar – “Sunday schools” for kids from 3-18 yrs of age
  • Chinmaya Yuva Kendra -“CHYK”- Youth(19-30 yrs) Spiritual & social service group
  • Study Groups, Devi groups – a weekly Vedanta forum for adults
  • Jnana Yajnas – Vedanta lecture series, seminars, and workshops for adults and CHYKs.
  • Spiritual Retreats and Camps – Enhanced scriptural learning and spiritual living
  • CM Seva – Social service projects
  • CM Swaranjali – the musical wing of the mission
  • CM Programs, Institutes and projects
  • CM Organization for rural Development (CORD) – Serving over 400 villages in India
  • CM Schools and Colleges – providing free and subsidized education
  • Chinmaya International Foundation- CIF (Research center for Sanskrit and Indology)
  • Intensive Vedanta institutes – Training for Chinmaya order of monks.
  • Pitamaha Sadans (senior citizens’ homes) – spiritually enriched living for seniors
  • Chinmaya Vibhooti project –CM vision and resource center
  • Classical music & dance school ( “NaadBindu” project)
  • Chinmaya Mission Alpharetta – Service to the Community

Chinmaya Mission Alpharetta started as a small balavihar (Sunday school) with 25 kids and 1 adult study group in Mar 2001. Currently we have about 600 kids with about 400 families that participate in the various programs. Our range of activities have multiplied to include many services like:

  • Language classes for kids (Hindi,Marathi,Tamil,Telugu,Kannada,Malyalam,Gujarati)
  • Youth Group (Social service projects mentored and lead by Middle & High school kids)
  • Adult Classes (Study groups, Chanting classes, Yoga)
  • Charitable projects (Food and toy drives, Feed the homeless.)
  • Events (Celebrations, Walkathons, competitions for various categories.)

CM Alpharetta has seen an immense growth and continues to touch and transform all connected with it.
